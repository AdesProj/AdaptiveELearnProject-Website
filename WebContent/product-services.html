Adaptive-Elearn Product and Services

b) Providing online training / e-learning services for Business Enterprises and professional institutions for Identified Courses – for staff development

2) – Hands-on Training and Lectureship on Identified Subjects/Courses: The practical training sessions and lecturing services include follow-up support for specific online course modules schedules identified for each learner or business enterprise or new customized / independent ICT courses requested by a learner/student, colleges and University department or community centre:

iii) Research and Development of "Cost Effective Adaptable E-Learning Tools"
