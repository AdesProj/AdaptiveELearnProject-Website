About Us

The ADES, Adaptive E-Learning: Online / E-Learning Services

The Adaptive E-Learn System (ADES) Ltd, a Spin-off company from recent research work at MBS, University of Manchester; (Ref: G. Vegah, 2011, Software Agent support for personalised learning: Negotiating and contracting with multiple providers).

ADES provides e-learning support software system, based on MOODLE open-source learning management system and intelligent software agent application; enables access to a range of online learning resources and multiple providers.

The products and services of ADES are designed to meet personalised and flexible learning for learners of diversified backgrounds, with multiple provider collaboration.

ADES provides ICT Courses to learners in community learning centres, students in College and staff development/training for various business institutions.

ADES is unique in providing Flexible personalised online Learning process, accessible on a range of "smart devices", at any time, and any where with multiple provider support options.

The ADES e-learning support systems, with customisable resources - open source and bespoke, presents users (learners/trainees, students, enterprise staff), with courses and resource options based on institution and personalised requirements; engaging learners in effective and most appropriate learning or training, with multiple collaborating providers.

a) The Products and Services of ADES product include:

i) Online E-learning and training courses - configuration of learning content management systems (LMS), and OER (Open-Educational Resources), based on intelligent software agent support processes;

ii) Online lectures and hands-on, support training sessions on a range of ICT Courses including - software systems development, web technology; embedded systems, amongst others;

iii) Research and Development of "Cost Effective Adaptable E-Learning Tools"

b) Why Clients Choose ADES

ADES has a team of professional and specialised staff in e-learning support system and believe in providing personalised tools and innovative learning approach that meets unique requirements of organisations and Individuals"; providing learning support where and when needed. With its Adaptable e-learning systems, ADES provides flexibility to learners/users, especially at community learning centres, colleges and remote regions to achieve certification, accreditation and portfolio's for a wide range of ICT courses, providing essential skills for employments and developing specialised knowledge/capacity on various subject of interest.

With ADES innovative learning technology, and research activity, ADES is developing state-of-the-art interactive and customisable e-learning tools that, not only supports development of specialised Skills and capacity building in a range of subjects; But also enables learning-provider collaboration, to meet personalised requirements.

Current ADES Collaboration and support service providers

ADES has a network of learning/training collaborators in ICT course content provision; ADES clients has access to a range of e-online / e-learning providers; and in addition to University modular Course accreditation, certification with institutions such as the British Computer Society (BCS), the International/European Computer Driving license (ICDL and ECDL); as well as identified University departments, for provision of online foundation courses, for community learning centres. ADES ensures available of its e-learning/distant learning and training courses and services in remote regions by partnering with dedicated broadband Services companies such as BT broadband and collaborating VSAT System providers, especially where demand for e-learning services is high and broadband internet access is absent or inadequate to support our online services.
